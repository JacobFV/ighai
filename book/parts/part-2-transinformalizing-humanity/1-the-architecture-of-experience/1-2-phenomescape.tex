\section{Phenomescapes}

Existance preceeds \definition{essence}{\textbf{Essence} is the intrinsic nature or indispensable quality of something, especially something abstract, which determines its character.}, but what follows is

the \definition{phenomescape}{\textbf{Phenomescape} is the perceptual landscape that we navigate through our senses. It is the world as it appears to us, as we experience it in the first person.}

By composition and grounding we host a network of symbols





\subsection{Essence Giving Meaning to Existence}
Existence without essence is barren. It's the essence that provides depth, meaning, and purpose. A song exists as a combination of notes, but its essence evokes emotions, memories, and experiences.
